Analysis

Surprisingly, the most popular genre among students was Action, followed closely by Horror and Sports games. This suggests that students enjoy fast-paced, exciting, and immersive experiences. On the other hand, RPGs were the least favored genre.

Some students preferred the same genre for different purposes. For example, Sports games were enjoyed by some for competition, while others played them for relaxation.
